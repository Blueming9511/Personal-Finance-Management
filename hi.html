Our process

thai • เกี่ยวกับเรา

Project preparation
Initial meetings and discussions
You will be in regular contact with our project manager via phone or video call to frame your needs. Understanding your wishes and your objectives is central to creating a result which delights you.
Planning the desired architecture and design (UX/UI)
We will work together on the architecture of your screens. Having studied your market and targets, we will deploy our UX expertise to offer a consistent user experience tailored to your needs.
Drafting detailed functional specifications
Once the screens have been approved, we will create a specification to detail each user functionality. This document will be the foundation of your project and will allow our developers to create complete technical documentation.
Launching the project and creating sprints
After finalizing the screens and the specifications, we will divide your project into sprints. Each sprint corresponds to a set of development tasks and ends with a period of internal and client-side tests. These stages make it possible to validate the project throughout its creation.
Development
Tracking progress
Once development has commenced, weekly reviews will be organized with our project manager. Precise progress tracking will allow you to organize your test phases as well as the arrival of the project on the market.
Testing and validation
At the end of each sprint, a test version is sent to the client. It is accompanied by specific documents which make it possible to verify all the screens and functions developed. This allows us to get your feedback and adjust our work as we go.
Final receipt and deployment
Once these steps are complete, you will be able to view your project in "beta version". This is our opportunity to adjust some last little details together before the long-awaited moment: the delivery of your site or your mobile application!
Project manintenance
Once the project is delivered, we don't abandon you! We will be there to accompany you during an agreed maintenance period. Our projects are designed to last, to deal with web and mobile updates, and to adapt to the evolution of your activity.
